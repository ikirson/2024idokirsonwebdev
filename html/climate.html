I would love to go to Namibia. However, as livestock and crop production decline, rural livelihood will be destroyed.

Current Impacts

Surface temperatures have risen 1.2 degrees celsius over the last century. The frequency of extreme temperatures has increased by 10%. Recent years have witness remarkably high temperatures, reaching levels they haven't in around 200-300 years.

Future Impacts

From 2046 to 2065 projections show that summer temperatures will rise between 1 and 3.5C. In that same time frame, winter temperatures can be expected to rise by 1 to 4C. Namibia's coastal areas are very vulnerable to rising sea levels, because saltwater will reach the freshwater drinking supply, compromising water availability.

Ways to Minimize the Impact of Climate Change

  • Carpool
  • Reduce, reuse, recycle
  • Limit food waste
  • Reduce meat consumption

Local Attempts to Mitigate Climate Change

  • Namibia's NCCSAP
  • Enhanced management of water resources in arid regions
  • Aiming to reduce greenhouse gas emissions
